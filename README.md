# EPUB to Audiobook Converter

Transform your e-books into audiobooks that you can listen to anywhere, anytime.

## What Does This Do?

Have you ever wanted to "read" a book while driving, exercising, or doing household chores? This tool takes your digital books (EPUB files) and turns them into audiobooks that you can listen to on your phone, computer, or any audio player.

Think of it as having a professional narrator read your entire book collection to you. You can enjoy your favorite novels, educational materials, or any text-based content in audio format, making reading accessible even when your hands and eyes are busy with other tasks.

## Key Features

### Turn Books into Audio

Simply choose an EPUB file from your device, and the tool will automatically read through the entire book and convert every chapter into spoken audio. It's like having someone read the book out loud for you, but without needing to hire a narrator.

### Choose Your Narrator's Voice

Pick from a variety of voice options to find the one you like best:
- **Male voices**: Choose from different speaking styles - some sound cool and detached, others warm and friendly, some have regional accents
- **Female voices**: Options range from sweet and gentle to professional and confident
- **English voices**: If your book is in English, there are native English-speaking voices available too

Each voice has its own personality and tone, so you can match the narrator to the type of book you're listening to.

### Complete Audiobook with Chapters

The converter creates a proper audiobook file (M4B format) that works just like professionally produced audiobooks:
- All chapters are organized and labeled
- You can jump between chapters easily
- Your listening position is saved automatically
- Works with popular audiobook apps and music players

### Flexible Output Options

**Single Complete Audiobook**: By default, all chapters are combined into one convenient file that you can transfer to any device and start listening immediately.

**Separate Chapter Files**: Prefer to have individual files for each chapter? You can choose to keep them separate, giving you more control over which parts to listen to.

### Smart Text Processing

The tool intelligently breaks down long chapters into manageable segments to ensure smooth, natural-sounding narration without overwhelming your device or compromising audio quality.

## Simple to Use

No technical knowledge required:
1. Select your EPUB book file
2. Choose a voice you like
3. Give your audiobook a name (or use the default)
4. Start the conversion and wait for it to complete
5. Enjoy your new audiobook!

The whole process is automatic once you make your selections. Just sit back and let the tool do the work while you focus on other things.
